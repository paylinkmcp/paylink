## What is PayLink Agent?

PayLink Agent is an AI-powered agent designed to help you understand everything related to payments in your PayLink account. Instead of manually going through logs and transaction data, the Agent analyzes your payment information, provides insights, and answers questions about your financial status, transaction patterns, and payment operations.

## Key Features

- **Payment Understanding**: Get answers to any question about your payments and transactions
- **Financial Status Analysis**: Understand your organization's financial status at a glance
- **Web Search Support**: Access web search capabilities for competitor analysis and market research
- **Log Analysis**: Automatically analyze logs and traces so you don't have to dig through them manually
- **Intelligent Insights**: Receive actionable insights about your payment operations

## Main Goal

The primary goal of PayLink Agent is to **eliminate the need to manually review logs and transaction data**. Instead of spending time searching through logs, traces, and transaction records, you can simply ask the Agent questions and get immediate, intelligent answers.

## Core Capabilities

### Payment Understanding

Ask the Agent anything about payments:

- **Transaction Questions**: "What was the total revenue last month?"
- **Payment Patterns**: "Which payment provider is used most frequently?"
- **Error Analysis**: "What payment errors occurred this week?"
- **Performance Metrics**: "What's the average transaction time for M-Pesa payments?"
- **Usage Statistics**: "How many STK Push operations were successful today?"

### Financial Status Analysis

Organizations can use the Agent to understand their financial status:

- **Revenue Overview**: Get summaries of revenue by project, time period, or payment provider
- **Transaction Trends**: Understand transaction patterns and trends over time
- **Balance Information**: Check wallet balances and fund movements
- **Financial Health**: Get insights into the financial health of your organization
- **Project Performance**: Compare financial performance across different projects

### Competitor Analysis with Web Search

The Agent includes web search support to help with competitor analysis:

- **Market Research**: Research competitors and market trends
- **Industry Insights**: Get information about payment industry developments
- **Best Practices**: Learn about industry best practices and standards
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Analyze competitor offerings and strategies

### Automatic Log Analysis

Instead of manually reviewing logs and traces:

- **Error Detection**: The Agent automatically identifies errors and issues
- **Pattern Recognition**: Detects patterns in transaction failures or successes
- **Anomaly Detection**: Flags unusual transaction patterns or behaviors
- **Summary Reports**: Provides summaries of log data without you having to read through everything

## How to Access PayLink Agent

### In the PayLink Dashboard

1. Log in to your PayLink account
2. Navigate to the Agent interface (typically in the sidebar or header)
3. Start asking questions about your payments, financial status, or any payment-related topic
4. The Agent will analyze your data and provide answers

## How It Works

1. **Ask a Question**: Type or speak your question about payments, financial status, or any related topic
2. **Data Analysis**: The Agent analyzes your PayLink data (logs, traces, transactions, wallets)
3. **Web Search** (if needed): For competitor analysis or market research, the Agent can search the web
4. **Intelligent Response**: Receive a clear, actionable answer based on your data and research

## Example Interactions

### Example 1: Financial Status

**You**: "What's the financial status of my organization this month?"

**Agent**: Analyzes your transaction data, wallet balances, and revenue streams, then provides a comprehensive financial summary with key metrics and insights.

### Example 2: Payment Analysis

**You**: "Why are M-Pesa transactions failing more than usual?"

**Agent**: Reviews error logs, traces, and transaction patterns, identifies the root cause, and suggests solutions.

### Example 3: Competitor Research

**You**: "How do our payment success rates compare to industry standards?"

**Agent**: Analyzes your transaction data and uses web search to find industry benchmarks, then provides a comparison.

### Example 4: Log Analysis

**You**: "What issues should I be concerned about from today's transactions?"

**Agent**: Automatically reviews all logs and traces from today, identifies issues, patterns, and anomalies, and presents a prioritized summary.
