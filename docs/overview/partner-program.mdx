## What is the Partner Program?

The PayLink Partner Program gives you exclusive early access to our comprehensive payment integration framework, allowing you to:

- **Build and test production-ready payment integrations** across M-Pesa, Airtel Money, Equity Bank, and KCB
- **Influence the roadmap** by providing direct feedback to our development team
- **Access premium support** from our engineering team
- **Shape the future** of AI-powered payment infrastructure for Africa
- **Get ahead of the competition** with early adoption of new features

## Your Responsibilities as a Partner

As a valued partner, we ask for your collaboration in making PayLink the best possible solution for the developer community:

- **Provide regular feedback** on PayLink features, functionality, and real-world usage
- **Report issues promptly** with detailed steps to reproduce and share error messages
- **Participate actively** in feedback sessions, surveys, and community discussions
- **Maintain confidentiality** by keeping access private within your organization
- **Test new features thoroughly** and confirm bug fixes and improvements

## What We're Looking For

We're interested in partners who are pushing the boundaries of AI-powered payments:

- **Innovative Use Cases**: Unique applications of PayLink technology in AI agent workflows
- **Integration Challenges**: How you solved complex technical problems with paylink
- **Performance Improvements**: Optimizations and best practices you've discovered
- **User Experience**: Creative approaches to payment flows and transaction handling
- **Provider Expansion**: Adding support for new payment providers or blockchain integrations

We're excited to showcase how our partners are using PayLink to build innovative payment solutions. If you'd like to share your story or be featured in our materials, please let us know!
